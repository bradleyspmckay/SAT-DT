Structured Analytic Techniques - Diagnosing Information

Scenario: Rising Tensions in the Republic of Anduria

The fictional country Anduria has been experiencing rising tensions with its neighbor, Braxia, over control of a disputed border region called the Tovak Corridor, which is rich in rare earth minerals. Over the past two months, troop movements, political rhetoric, and cyber activity have increased on both sides.

Intelligence Requirement

Is the Republic of Anduria preparing for a cross-border military incursion into the Tovak Corridor within the next 30 days?

Intelligence Reporting

Report One: Social Media Post

Source: Unverified Twitter account claiming to be a local resident near the border
Date: 4 days ago
Content: “Can hear helicopters flying low near my village every night now. Something's going on. I saw tanks 3 days ago moving east. First time in years! #Anduria #TovakTension”

Report Two: HUMINT Source A

Source: A mid-level Andurian military officer with a history of accurate reporting
Date: 1 day ago
Content: “An armored brigade has been ordered to stage near the border within the week. The directive came from General Kael himself. Officers were told to prepare for a ‘readiness inspection,’ but several units were issued live ammunition.”

Report Three: SIGINT Intercept

Source: Intercepted phone call between two Braxian military planners
Date: 2 days ago
Content: “...they’ve been running drills, but I don’t think they’re crazy enough to try something before the trade summit. Not while the UN is still watching.”

Report Four: News Article from Andurian State Media

Source: Anduria National Herald (state-run news outlet)
Date: 3 days ago
Content: “President Varek assures citizens: ‘We will defend every inch of Andurian soil. The Tovak Corridor is, and always has been, ours.’ Parade planned to celebrate Armed Forces Day this weekend with armored vehicle demonstration in East Province.”

Report Five: Satellite Imagery Analysis

Source: Imagery from commercial satellite provider, analyzed by NGA contractor
Date: 2 days ago
Content: “Recent imagery (July 29) shows the deployment of 40+ armored vehicles and 15 mobile artillery pieces to staging area 14 km from Tovak border. Activity is consistent with pre-deployment posture. Presence of fuel tankers suggests sustained operations.”
